## Research Focus

I specialize in the intersection of artificial intelligence and atmospheric sciences, with a focus on:

### 🎯 AI-Enhanced Weather Forecasting
Developing deep learning models to improve the accuracy and efficiency of traditional numerical weather prediction, with emphasis on short-term nowcasting and medium-range forecasting.

### 🌍 Climate Model Emulation and Downscaling
Using machine learning methods to accelerate climate simulations and enhance the spatiotemporal resolution of regional climate predictions.

### ⚡ Extreme Weather Detection and Early Warning
Implementing deep learning algorithms based on satellite and radar data for automatic identification and early warning of extreme weather events.

### 🧠 Foundation Models for Meteorology
Building pre-trained foundation models for the meteorological domain to support multi-task learning and few-shot learning.

## About My Research

I develop and apply artificial intelligence techniques to improve weather forecasting accuracy and enhance climate model performance. My work focuses on:

- **Deep Learning for Numerical Weather Prediction**: Developing neural network architectures that complement traditional physics-based models
- **Climate Data Analysis**: Using machine learning to identify patterns and trends in climate datasets
- **Extreme Weather Detection**: Creating AI systems for early warning of severe meteorological events
- **Data Assimilation**: Improving the integration of observational data into forecasting systems
