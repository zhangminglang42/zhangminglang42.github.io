## Teaching Philosophy

My teaching approach emphasizes:

1. **Practical Application**: Connecting theoretical concepts to real-world atmospheric science problems
2. **Hands-on Learning**: Providing opportunities for students to work with actual meteorological data
3. **Interdisciplinary Integration**: Bridging computer science and atmospheric sciences
4. **Research-Informed Teaching**: Incorporating cutting-edge research findings into course content
5. **Inclusive Pedagogy**: Creating accessible learning environments for students with diverse backgrounds
